Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Food Bridge

Donating food is easy! Simply click on the "Donate Food" button on our homepage, fill out the donation form with details about the food you'd like to donate, and our team will arrange for pickup at your convenience.

We accept all types of edible, non-perishable and perishable food that is safe for consumption. This includes cooked meals, raw ingredients, packaged foods, and produce. We cannot accept food that is spoiled, moldy, or past its expiration date.

Your food donation will be distributed to individuals and families in need through our network of partner organizations, including homeless shelters, orphanages, community centers, and disaster relief programs. We ensure that all donated food reaches those who need it most.

Absolutely! We welcome volunteers who are passionate about fighting hunger and reducing food waste. You can help with food collection, sorting, distribution, event organization, and administrative tasks. Visit our "Get Involved" page to learn more and sign up as a volunteer.
