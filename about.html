About FoodBridge

Our mission to fight hunger and reduce food waste

Our Story

Food Bridge began with a simple realization: while millions go hungry, tons of edible food is wasted every day. We're here to bridge that gap.

Our network of volunteers and partner organizations ensures that this food reaches those who need it most - homeless shelters, orphanages, low-income families, and disaster-stricken communities.

Our Values

Principles that guide our work

Compassion

We approach our work with empathy and care for those we serve.

Sustainability

We're committed to environmental stewardship and reducing waste.

Community

We believe in the power of collective action and collaboration.

Integrity

We operate with transparency and accountability in all we do.
